## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I use the free version in production?
A: The Developer Plan (free) is for development and testing only. For production use, you need a paid license.

### Q: What happens if I exceed my node limit?
A: You'll receive a notification to upgrade. Service continues uninterrupted for 30 days, then nodes beyond your limit will be disabled.

### Q: Do you offer refunds?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for first-time customers. Contact support for details.

### Q: Can I switch between plans?
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades at your next billing cycle.

### Q: Do you offer on-premises deployment?
A: Yes, all our plans support on-premises deployment. We also offer managed cloud hosting.

### Q: What support do I get with each plan?
A: Support levels vary by plan. See the comparison table above for details.

### Q: Can I get a custom SLA?
A: Yes, enterprise customers can negotiate custom SLAs. Contact sales for details.

### Q: Do you offer training?
A: Yes, we offer training for all plans. Enterprise plans include on-site training.

### Q: What's your data retention policy?
A: We retain your data for the duration of your subscription plus 90 days for backup purposes.

### Q: Do you offer migration assistance?
A: Yes, we offer migration assistance from other consensus systems. Contact sales for a quote.
